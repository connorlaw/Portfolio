Case study

Bespoke Wordpress theme
for travel blog start-up

Introduction

As a developer with a passion for both technology and travel, I saw an exciting opportunity to combine them into a project that was both meaningful and challenging.

My wife, Hannah, and I have always been avid travellers, and after our unforgettable six-month honeymoon adventure across Asia, we felt compelled to share our experiences, tips, and itineraries with a wider audience. This led to the creation of our travel blog, Laws of Travel, where we document our journeys and provide valuable insights to fellow travellers.

The site needed to be a reflection of our personalities, easy to navigate, visually engaging, and flexible enough to grow with our content. The challenge was to build a platform that could seamlessly combine our stories, photography, and travel advice in a way that was both user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing.


Solution

To bring Laws of Travel to life, I handled the full design and development process. Starting with Figma, I crafted a design that was clean, modern, and reflective of our travel style. The design focused on high-quality imagery, intuitive navigation, and a layout that could accommodate various types of content, such as blog posts and travel itineraries.

Once the design was finalised, I moved on to development, choosing WordPress as the platform due to its flexibility and scalability. Wanting full control of the underlying architecture, I built a custom theme from scratch. This allowed me to ensure that the website was optimised for performance, mobile-friendly, and tailored specifically to our needs. The custom theme also made it easier to implement unique features that could easily be updated as our travels continued.

Throughout the development process, I focused on creating a seamless user experience, ensuring that visitors could easily find the content they were looking for, whether it was a detailed itinerary or a quick travel tip.

By combining my technical expertise with our shared passion for travel, I was able to create a platform that is both functional and visually appealing. The site has received positive feedback from users who appreciate its design, ease of use, and the quality of content.

As we continue to explore new destinations, Laws of Travel will evolve with us, providing a space where we can share our journey with the world. This project has been a rewarding experience, blending creativity and technology to create something that is both personal and professional.

Case study

Leading frontend development
for a growing UK estate agent

Introduction

With a fresh design in hand, Moss & Co hired me to handle development of the frontend of their client portal. Looking to get the project completed as soon as possible, they leaned on my expertise to advise and deliver the best possible frontend interface.


Solution

Due to the emphasis on pace of delivery, I initially investigated the options that would allow me to fulfil this desire, eventually opting to utilise the Bootstrap library in order to put together a consistent and high-quality interface at speed.

With the designs to hand, I was able to build the frontend pages and components - including modals, toasts, accordions, etc - while incorporating dummy PHP data that would later be filled in by the backend developer.


Outcome

I'm very happy with the quality of work provided, taking immense care to deliver consistent, responsive and brand-aligned templates for the project. Built to be extendable and easy to pick up by any developer, I believe the platform is future-proofed, and miles ahead of where it was before.

Details

Fast frontend delivery using Bootstrap for Moss & Co's newly designed real estate client portal.

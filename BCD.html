How can we help?

We offer our smooth and efficient business to private individuals, companies and local government in the disposal of all cars and light commercial vehicles.

Give us a call and we can usually arrange collection within 24 hours.

If you're interested in parts, you're in luck!

We have three acres of vehicles and can supply used parts for a wide range of popular makes and models - body parts, starters, coded radios unlocked, radios for sale... you name it!

Whatever you're looking for, give us a call to find if we have it, then come on over. Our friendly staff are on hand to give you assistance.
